# ba2020_564925 textadventure

Hi and welcome to my small and short textadventure.

Here you can create your own character, and explore an adventure.
You yourself decide what will happen so try to stay safe.
Follow the story and see how it unfolds!

# Usage

Upon starting, you will be asked to choose a name (please don't choose something real names or obscene / inappropriate),
distribute skill points, and to choose an appearance.
After that you can start the game, follow the story and decided what choices your character will make. Enjoy!
